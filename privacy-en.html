Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-01-23

1. Introduction

Welcome to LogicFun! We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

By using the LogicFun app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage, game statistics, feature usage frequency
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, performance metrics, error logs
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage statistics via Firebase Analytics
  • Advertising Identifiers: Device advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) for displaying ads

2.2 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • ‚ùå Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • ‚ùå Location information
  • ‚ùå Contacts or photos
  • ‚ùå Any sensitive personal data

2.3 Locally Stored Information

The following information is stored only on your device and not uploaded to servers:

  • Game progress and saves
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Game statistics and achievements
  • App configuration

3. Information Use

We use collected information to:

  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns, optimize user experience
  • Fix Issues: Identify and fix app crashes and errors
  • Optimize Performance: Monitor app performance, improve efficiency
  • Develop Features: Understand user needs, develop new features

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting
  • Firebase Performance: Performance monitoring

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following information:

  • Advertising Identifiers: Device advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • Ad Interaction Data: Ad impressions, clicks

Ad Personalization:

  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.3 Data Transfer

  • All data transfers use encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Data is stored on Google Cloud servers (GDPR compliant)

5. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • Industry-standard encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Data minimization principles
  • Anonymized analytics data

6. Data Retention

  • Usage Data: Automatically deleted after 60 days
  • Crash Reports: Automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Local Data: Can be deleted anytime by uninstalling the app

7. Children's Privacy

LogicFun is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View collected data (via in-app statistics)
  • Delete: Remove all locally stored data (uninstall app)
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics in app settings
  • Complain: File complaints with data protection authorities

9. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app. Continued use indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
